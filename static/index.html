Animal Shogi is a board game. The aim is to capture the opponent's lion, or get your lion to the end of the board!

On your turn, you can move a piece one square, in a direction shown by its red dots.

If you capture an opponent's piece, it goes into your bench. Instead of moving a piece, you can play a piece from your bench anywhere on the board.

If your chick moves to the end of the board, it becomes a more powerful hen!
